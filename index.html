Director's Message

Welcome to Sultan Hanafi Royal Schools

My name is Zakariya Olanrewaju Anofi, and I am delighted to welcome you to the official home of Sultan Hanafi Royal Schools — the best hybrid, Islamic and secular, school in Lagos, Nigeria, located in Ikorodu. As Director, I am deeply committed to ensuring that every student receives the highest quality education in a nurturing, safe, and inclusive environment.

"Ensuring that every student receives the highest quality education in a nurturing, safe, and inclusive environment."

My over two decades of work experience span banking, insurance, oil & gas, and consulting, with a bias for corporate reporting, taxation management, revenue generation, financial and business advisory, governance, and leadership. My passion for education informed my decision to invest in the creation of institutions whose sole motive is to impart knowledge to the populace.

Our schools are open to Muslims and non-Muslims, males and females. At SULTAN, our dedicated team of educators are passionate about equipping students with the skills, knowledge, and values they need to succeed in an ever-changing business and technology world. Whether you are a prospective student, a current parent, or an alumni member, we are here to support and guide you.

Zakariya Olanrewaju Anofi
